  • Principal Investigator
    • 2020-Present: Assistant Professor, City of hope (will start from June 29, 2020)
    • 2015-2020: Postdoctoral Research Associate, St Jude Children's Research Hospital
    • 2008-2014: Ph.D Student, Shanghai Jiao Tong University

    Dr. Zhaohui Gu received his bachelor's degree in biomedical engineering from Xi'an Jiao Tong University in 2008, then he joined Dr. Zhu Chen's laboratory for his Ph.D. training at Shanghai Institute of Hematology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. After receiving a Ph.D. degree in bioinformatics, he joined Dr. Charles Mullighan's group at St. Jude Children's Research Hospital as a postdoctoral fellow in 2015. His research has led to 13 first/co-first authored publications on Nature, Nature Genetics, Nature Communications, Bioinformatics, PNAS, etc. He has been supported by the ASH Scholar Award, LLS Special Fellow Award, and NIH/NCI K99/R00 Award. From June 29th, 2020, Dr. Gu will set up his own lab at City of Hope National Medical Center as an assistant professor.
